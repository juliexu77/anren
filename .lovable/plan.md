

## Remove the Daily Orientation Card

The "Good morning. 6 things resting here… View calendar →" block is the **orientation card** rendered in `HomeView.tsx`. It's generated by `src/lib/dailyOrientation.ts`.

### Changes

1. **`src/components/HomeView.tsx`** — Remove the entire `orientation-card` `<div>` block (the one rendering `orientationLines` and the "View calendar →" button). Also remove the related imports/state: `generateDailyOrientation`, `OrientationLine`, `handleOrientationTap`, and `orientationLines`.

2. **`src/lib/dailyOrientation.ts`** — Delete the file (no longer used anywhere).

3. **`src/components/HomeView.tsx` props** — Remove `onViewCalendar`, `onCalendarEventClick`, `calendarEvents`, and `calendarLoading` from the Props interface if they're only used by the orientation card. (Will verify during implementation — some may still be needed by the calendar agenda button in Index.tsx.)

This keeps the "Run My Day" AI plan, action buttons, and "Resting here" list intact — just removes that static orientation greeting.
